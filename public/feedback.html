Requests and reviews

Help improve Digital Nomad Japan.

We welcome content requests, correction notes, source suggestions, local updates, and usability feedback from readers planning remote-work stays in Japan.

Feedback form in preparation

A public feedback form is being prepared. Until a secure form is connected, this page explains what kind of feedback is useful and what private information should not be sent.

What to share

Useful requests and reviews

Content request

Ask for a new guide

Request topics such as eSIMs, coworking, housing, banking, family stays, tax basics, or a specific city.

Correction

Report outdated information

Point to the page that may need review and include a public source URL that supports the correction.

Source suggestion

Share official or local sources

Suggest government pages, city programs, safety sources, destination reports, or practical local references.

Site review

Review the user experience

Tell us whether the site feels clear, trustworthy, mobile-friendly, easy to navigate, and useful for planning.

How feedback helps

How requests may be used

Prioritize new pages

Repeated requests can help decide which guides, cities, and practical topics should be written next.

Review existing guidance

Correction notes and public sources can help identify pages that need fact checks, clearer language, or new review dates.

Improve site structure

Usability reviews can help improve navigation, page hierarchy, mobile layout, and calls to action.

Privacy and safety

Do not send private documents

Please do not send passport numbers, visa documents, tax records, bank details, medical information, insurance documents, employment contracts, or urgent personal legal questions. Digital Nomad Japan does not provide immigration, tax, legal, medical, insurance, or emergency advice.

  • If the feedback is about a visa or rule, send the public source URL, not your private case documents.
  • If your situation is urgent, contact the appropriate authority, embassy, consulate, emergency service, or qualified professional.
  • If your question depends on personal legal, tax, medical, insurance, or immigration facts, seek professional advice.
