## Recommended Topic

**How to pay in Japan without relying on a Japanese bank account**

Core message: most short-stay digital nomads should prepare a resilient payment setup using foreign cards, accessible international ATMs, and some cash rather than assuming they can immediately open a local bank account.

Fact-check basis:

- JNTO states that international credit, debit, and prepaid cards are generally accepted, but some situations still require cash.
- JNTO identifies Japan Post Bank and Seven Bank ATMs as withdrawal options for international cards.
- Japan Post Bank confirms withdrawals with eligible cards issued by overseas financial institutions, subject to card, fee, limit, and operating-hour conditions.
